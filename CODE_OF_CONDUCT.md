# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, technical, and focused on improving the project.

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being clear and constructive in feedback.
- Respecting different levels of experience.
- Assuming good intent while still being precise about problems.
- Keeping legal and safety boundaries intact.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, publishing private information, or encouraging misuse of the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior makes the project unsafe or unproductive.

## Scope

This code applies to project spaces including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and community channels associated with Resonance Graph.
